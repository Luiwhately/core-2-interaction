How?

by Lui Whately

1

A little girl wakes up on a lazy Saturday and no one else is awake. She didn’t know what to do, she could watch TV, but she couldn’t reach the TV remote control.

2

Suddenly the computer in the adult's office made a noise. The sound intrigued her, so she went to check, what could the noise be?

3

The girl clicked on the keyboard, got into the emails tab, and wrote her name in the destination tab. Accidentally selects an email destination to send. Who was it for? She had no idea and also didn’t care. She right down: “ hi, it’s Luiza, who is there?”

4

Press send

5

Hours later her mom received a call from the girls grandmother, also called Luiza, trying to understand what was going on.
