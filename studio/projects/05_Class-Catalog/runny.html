This website gives the user ideas of paths they could take to explore NYC. The design expresses an artistic way to represent the tracks without losing the easy use of the website.

The user can navigate and filter the specificities they want for their journey, such as how many miles, the starting location, personal evaluation of the designer, and more.

In addition, the user can also have access to the GoogleMaps embed information on the pathway on each track page.
