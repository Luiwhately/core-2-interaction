Union Square to Hudson River and 5av

About the track:

Miles: 4.7

Rating: 4/5

This is not a looped track, starting at Union Square Park and finishing at 5av

About the track:

On Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays, Union Square Park hosts the Farmers Market flea, which is one of my favorite spots to get fresh vegetables and fruits. A nice place to choose a fruit before your run! After that, this track takes you to the Hudson River and later for a tour around the beautiful streets in West Village.
