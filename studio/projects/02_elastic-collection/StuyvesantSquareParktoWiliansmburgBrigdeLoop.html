Stuyvesant Square Park to Williansburg Bridge

About the track:

Miles: 6

Rating: 4/5

This is a looped track starting at Stuyvesant Square Park in the Lower Eastside and crossing the Williasnburg Bridge back and forward

About the track:

I love running across bridges! The Williasnburg Bridge has a pedestrial lane, a bike lane, a care lane and a subway line that passes by. Definetely a lot going on! But the view is vevry pretty! The way to get to the bridge is not my favorite are to run, as the sidewalks are narrow, but it is okay!
